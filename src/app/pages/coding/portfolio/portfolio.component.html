When I started this project, I had no experience or knowledge of web development. As such, the first thing I did was to learn the basics of HTML, CSS, and TypeScript. Once I felt comfortable I did research on the tech stack I wanted to use.

The first big decision I had to make was what framework I wanted to use. During my research the two frameworks that stood out to me were Angular and React. I decided to go with angular because I liked the idea of having everything in one place. Another reason I chose Angular was because I preferred the idea of having separate Typescript and HTML files as opposed to TSX. I also decided to use SCSS because I like having for loops in my CSS code.
